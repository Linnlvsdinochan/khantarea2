Travel to Cancún

Cancún haves one of the most beautiful beaches in Latinamerica, and its hotels have a really pretty sea view, with tall plam trees

THINGS THAT YOU CAN DO

  • Go to a seafood restaurant
  • Play in the sand
  • Swim in the pool
  • Go to the Hard Rock Cafe near the hotel zone
  • Go to a club and have fun :)

Like I said before you can go to the hotel zone, there's a lot of nocturnal live, clubs, outlets, where you can have a lot of fun with your friends or your couple.

On the other side, if you are doing a familiar travel, you can find little traditional shops, these shops sells distinctive mexican objects like

  • The Mexican flag
